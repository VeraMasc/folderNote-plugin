# Comically Answered Questions

In case you have a question or just want to laugh at my jokes.

## Trail

*"Why is the trail not integrated in the way obsidian already shows you the note's path?"*

Because I will definitely break something without realizing. Maybe not now, maybe not for my current workflow, maybe not with the other plugins I use, but it feels like asking for something bad to happen. In general, I try to write my plugins to be as robust and as low maintenance as possible, because I don't know if I'll have the time to fix stuff when an update breaks something and I can't afford to have the way I use to move through my vault just stop working.

I hate useless redundancy and this is a perfect example of it, but I could be doing much better things with my time. Believe it or not, making this plugin is supposed to make me more productive, not less.

## Bookmarks

*"But why make this when you can simply not close the tab and continue later?"*

Because, my dear strawman, my phone's battery manager loves to close Obsidian the moment I stop looking at my screen, Microsoft restarts my computer without my consent and things go really bad the moment I start opening tabs in any program. I must have been a memory leak in my past life the way I refuse to free any memory.
